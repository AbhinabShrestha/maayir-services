OUR CAPABILITIES

Our Comprehensive Services

Every division operates under rigorous workflows to secure seamless results, regulatory structural alignment, and premium luxury finishes.

1. Engraving & Ornamentation Works

Bespoke intricate relief carvings, architectural motifs, gypsum accents, and high-end cultural designs crafted by masters.

2. Electromechanical Equipment Installation & Maintenance

Industrial and heavy asset assemblies, systematic alignments, switchgear setups, and lifecycle preventative operations.

3. Carpentry & Wood Flooring Works

Premium parquet assemblies, customized fit-outs, luxury cabinetry installations, and structural timber framework finishing.

4. Sanitary Installation & Pipes Repairing

Advanced copper/PPR network design, diagnostic high-pressure leakage resolution, and luxury sanitary integration.

5. Floor & Wall Tiling Works

Laser-guided layouts for luxury marble, large-format porcelain slabs, and glass mosaics with structural water-tight grouting.

6. Plumbing & Sanitary Installation

Comprehensive greywater infrastructure, drainage engineering, and elite booster pump installations for high-rise or villa frameworks.

7. Air-Conditioning, Ventilations & Air Filtration Systems

Precision Chiller, VRF, and FCU engineering, chemical sanitization, and high-efficiency HEPA air filter lifecycle updates.

8. False Ceiling & Light Partitions Installation

Acoustic panel balancing, fire-rated gypsum board drywall designs, and hidden shadow-gap profile framing.

9. Electrical Fittings & Fixtures Repairing & Maintenance

Load balancing calculations, luxury smart illumination integrations, distribution board upgrades, and detailed safety audits.

10. Building Cleaning Services

Post-construction deep sanitization, exterior glass cleaning, and commercial-grade high-pressure facility detailing.

11. Plaster Works

High-caliber rendering, Venetian plaster overlays, decorative stucco, and absolute flush alignment of uneven base blockworks.

12. General Maintenance & Renovation

Full turnkey residential asset restorations, comprehensive structural modernizations, and responsive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC).
